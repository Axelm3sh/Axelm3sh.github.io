# Summer Haze

I wasn't planning to redesign anything. like ever.

Then I looked at a can of soda. Then I looked at the thermometer. Then some music channel was on in the background...it shouldn't have set the mood, but it did for a weekend project.

Next thing I know: 🏖️ and manga stuff. Complete 180 from whatever I had before lol

Felt fun, if it works, it works. If your eyes melt (like I did in the heat), then I did my job correctly.
